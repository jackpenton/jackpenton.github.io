ABOUT

Jack Penton is an aspiring writer, photographer and filmmaker. His work aims to combat issues among humanity and the environment. Based in Reading, Berkshire he investigates topics around the town such as homelessness, drug abuse and knife crime. Eventually his goal is to move further afield and focus on widespread cases that are a global concern. This includes climate change, human rights and animal welfare.
